About Our Project

Our project, Crop Disease Predictor, leverages advanced machine learning techniques to help farmers identify crop diseases from images. By simply uploading a photo of the crop, users can receive predictions about the disease, its description, and possible treatments. This tool aims to empower farmers with timely information to improve crop health and productivity.
